Rotating Weights

2025
To Transport Time Exhibition
Ho-Gae Train Station, Ulsan, South Korea

Industrial statistics from Ulsan are transformed into sound and visuals. Scales set at different heights respond to weight with shifting pie charts and vocal tones, turning abstract labour data into an intergenerational rhythm.

LED display, headphones, electronic weights, LED lights, dimensions Variable

Void && Form

2025
Artificial Territories Exhibition
Das LOT, Vienna (Team Mangshinsal)

A facial recognition system analyses viewers' expressions and generates music based on perceived emotion. Digital twins, algorithmic text, and spinning mandalas humorously reveal the gap between confident analysis and emotional misreading.

Projector, stereo sound, webcam, dimensions variable

Metal Rave

2024
The Six Isles Exhibition
Dongbin 1969, Pohang

Heavy metal contamination data from the Hyeongsan River is rendered as navigable sound. Using a joystick to explore the map, visitors encounter unique sonic textures tied to pollution levels, making invisible toxicity audible.

Conveyor Belt, 4.1 surround sound system, Max/MSP, dimensions variable
